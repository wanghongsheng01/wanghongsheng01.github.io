Founder Hongsheng Wang, who holds a Ph.D. from Zhejiang University, leads a team that has independently developed a fully domestic AI training and inference engine. This includes distributed training and inference tools specifically designed for large models.

The team's core technical strengths lie in the end-to-cloud collaborative deployment of large models on edge devices, enabling chip manufacturers to rapidly build software stacks and providing commercial AI large model inference tools for edge applications. Originating from Zhejiang University, the core team is a dedicated AI infrastructure R&D group with deep expertise in compiler and large model inference engine development. Key members have contributed to the development of software stacks for various chips, including Huawei Ascend, Tianshu Zhixin, and Cambricon.
